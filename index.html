Chef-d'œuvre Ala Carte

Ratatouille, a film by the illustrious Pixar Animation Studio of the 2000s, is about a rat being able to cook. Many doubt this ability, some humans, some rats too, but Remy doesn't falter. He continues to show everyone that ANYONE can cook.


A Rat Can Cook, Anyone Can Cook.

Remy is a rat, so this undoubtedly affects how his ability to cook is seen. Rats are assosciated with filth and trash, so who would want a rat to cook? Remy is hit with these doubts, but it doesn't make him falter in his dream. His dream to be a cook. This story brings us along his journey to see how ANYONE can cook, and by extension, ANYONE can follow their dream. Its moral is to not let the voices of others hinder you from pursuing your dream.
